Help & Commands

Wake Words

Start your command with one of these:

  • "Wolfgang, ..."
  • "Mozart, ..."

Music Commands

  • Play: "Wolfgang, play"
  • Pause: "Mozart, pause"
  • Select Song: "Wolfgang, play Queen"
  • Restart: "Mozart, restart song"
  • Rewind: "Wolfgang, go back 10 seconds"
  • Fast Forward: "Mozart, skip ahead 30 seconds"
  • Speed Up: "Wolfgang, faster"
  • Slow Down: "Mozart, slower"

Tips

  • Speak clearly and wait for the wake word to be detected
  • Commands work best in a quiet environment
  • You can use either "Wolfgang" or "Mozart" as the wake word
  • The system continuously listens - no need to keep clicking the button
